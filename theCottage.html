The Cottage

It is a three bedroom cottage featuring modern living and original features such as beamed ceilings, sleeping up to 6 people. The accommodation comprises two double and one twin bedroom, two bathrooms, living area, dining area and kitchen.

To the rear of the house there is a raised area comprising a patio with BBQ and firepit set in a wildflower meadow.

The house is bright, airy, and snug having recently been insulated to the highest standards. There is oil fired central heating, a wood burning stove, satellite television and WIFI.

Outside is a small shed for storing bikes etc and the washing machine and freezer are also located here.
