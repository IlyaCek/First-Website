The Locality

The beaches of Port-na-Blagh and Marble Hill are within easy driving distance or can be reached on foot in approx 15 and 25 minutes respectively. The walk to the Blue Flag Marble Hill beach features spectacular scenery and coffee, snacks and ice cream are available at the Shack on arrival.

Situated in Port-na-Blagh are the Cove Restaurant and Buttermilk Cafe and Art Gallery as well as the Green Door Studio & Gallery. For those interested in fishing there is angling from Port-na-Blagh Pier and lake fishing at Sessiagh Lake.

The Shandon at Marble Hill is a full facility hotel with restaurant, luxury spa, and swimming pool.
